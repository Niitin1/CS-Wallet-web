What is CREDITS?

CREDITS is a new digital cryptocurrency and also a payment system, built on the principles of blockchain. It works without a central bank or single administrator, acting as a peer-to-peer decentralized network. The highest level of security is based on the principles of cryptography.

The CREDITS cryptocurrency is a new unique technological development characterized by having a very high speed of payment execution (averages less than a second) and a low fee per transaction. This is a completely new independent platform development by the CREDITS project team.

All details about transactions occuring between addresses on the system are available to view. Using the service Monitor (http://monitor.credits.com) you can verify a transaction. Electronic payment between two parties takes place without intermediaries and is irreversible — there is no mechanism to cancel the confirmed transaction.

The CREDITS Wallet is a browser-based application executable on the user's browser-side. All data is stored and processed in the user's browser & transmitted directly to the network. By doing this, data is not stored on any server. In the case of a password-access key access being lost, the account will be lost.

On the CREDITS platform it is possible through programming to implement options of smart contracts for the inssurance of tokens and the creation of various services built on the principles of blockchain.
